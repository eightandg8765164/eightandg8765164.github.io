HUNGER GAMES

The Hunger Games universe is a dystopia set in Panem, a country consisting of the wealthy Capitol and 12 districts in varying states of poverty.

READ MORE

The novels were all well received. In August 2012, the series ranked second, beaten only by the Harry Potter series in NPR's poll of the top 100 teen novels, which asked voters to choose their favorite young adult books. On August 17, 2012, Amazon announced The Hunger Games trilogy as its top seller, surpassing the record previously held by the Harry Potter series.

Main Characters

Katniss Everdeen

She is quiet, independent, and fierce. She has long dark hair, olive skin, and grey eyes, which are given as characteristic of residents of the coal mining region of District 12 known as "the Seam".She lives with her mother and younger sister, Primrose.

Peeta Mellark

Peeta has been in love with Katniss ever since he first saw her during elementary school, and declares his love for her during the pre-Games interview.His skills include personal charm and charisma, physical strength, baking, and painting.
